About Us

Solano Hillside Residences is a thoughtfully planned, eco-friendly development by Phinma Properties. Located in Muntinlupa near Alabang, it offers modern hillside living with scenic elevation, natural light, and serene surroundings.

Designed for young professionals and growing families, Solano is more than just a place to live — it’s a community rooted in comfort, connection, and opportunity.
